Overview

This project presents a Virtual Fabrication Lab (Fab Lab) Tour developed using virtual reality technologies. The system allows users to explore a fab lab environment interactively, similar to navigating through Google Maps. Users can experience a realistic layout of the lab without physically being present.

The platform integrates detailed 3D models of semiconductor manufacturing machines along with informative videos that demonstrate their functionality and purpose. This combination of visual and interactive elements enhances user understanding of complex fabrication processes.

The project serves as an effective application of virtual reality by providing remote access to specialized facilities that are typically restricted due to safety and operational constraints.
